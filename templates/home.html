About

Knitting, crochet and sewing make us feel good. It helps us focus, feel creative and put the stresses of the day to rest. Now we want to share this 'secret' - it's the perfect time to get involved.

Make yourself happy!

Stitching Life is a place for knitters, crocheters and sewers to keep update to new patterns, and look to others for ideas and inspiration. The content here is all user- driven. This is a great place for you to keep notes about your projects, see what other people are making and find the perfect pattern.
